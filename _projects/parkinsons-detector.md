Designed an audio classification pipeline for Parkinson's disease detection from vowel phonation.

- Built a transformer-based approach for clinical audio signals.
- Reached approximately 75% ROC-AUC on the mPower dataset.
- Outperformed the CNN baseline by roughly 15%.
